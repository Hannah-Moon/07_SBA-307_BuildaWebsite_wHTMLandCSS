About

Invest together to support mission


Seeing the challenges faced by nonprofits firsthand, I understand how crucial it is for them to have ongoing support.

They want to focus on their mission of making a positive impact, but it's tough without reliable funds.

That's why I'm creating a web app where the community can invest together, providing ongoing help to Pittsburgh's nonprofits.

Let's make a real difference, ensuring our beloved organizations have what they need to continue their important!

Impact

Prove with a number

Picture this: your investment impact is measured in real-time, giving you the confidence that your support is making a difference as soon as you click.

With our platform, your donations reach organizations instantly, ensuring they receive crucial funding exactly when it's needed. It's about trust, transparency, and transforming lives together.
